CONECTA_4

Welcome to CONECTA_4 game!

The game starts with a blank panel with 42 positions, organized in 7 columns of 6 positions each.

The RED player is the first one to play, the BLUE player comes right after.

The player must choose a column to put his piece, the piece will always be put on the bottom of the column where the position is not taken by another piece.

To win, the player must connect 4 pieces of it's own color in any direction (diagonals are good too).
